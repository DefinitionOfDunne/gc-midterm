Once upon a time in the mystical land of Shangrila there dwelt an immortal named Kai-Yun and his trusty band of ninja monkeys.
Unbeknownst to many, the ninja monkeys were dedicated to only one thing more than Kai-Yun... Banana milk.
However resources were scarce, and the banana milk was in short supply due to deforestation.  They competed with their mortal foes, the kung-fu sloths, for the precious elixer.
